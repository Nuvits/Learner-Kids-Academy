# **Learner Kids Academy**
## **Privacy Policy-EN**
Last Updated: December 2, 2025

At Learner Kids Academy ("we", "the app"), we take our users' privacy seriously. This privacy policy explains what information our application collects and how it uses it.

### **1.Collected Information**
The Learner Kids Academy app collects no personal data.
* ❌ Name, email, or phone number is not collected
* ❌ Location information is not collected
* ❌ Device information is not collected
* ❌ Usage statistics are not collected
* ❌ Cookies are not used

### **2.Data Storage**
The app only stores the following information locally on your device:\
* Language preference (Turkish/English)
* Sound settings (On/Off)

This information remains only on your device and is not sent to any server.

### **3. Internet Connection**
The application does not require an internet connection and works offline.

### **4. Third-Party Services**
The app uses no third-party services:
* ❌ No Google Analytics
* ❌ No Firebase
* ❌ No Ad networks
* ❌ No Social media integration

### **5. Children's Privacy**
Our app is designed for children aged 3-8 and is COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act) compliant.

* No information is collected from children
* No parental consent required
* Works completely safely and offline

### **6. Data Security**
Since no data is collected, the risk of a data breach is zero.

### **7. Changes**
This privacy policy may be updated. Changes will be posted on this page.
